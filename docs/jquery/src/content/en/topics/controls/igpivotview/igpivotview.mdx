# igPivotView

## In This Group of Topics

### Introduction

The topics in this group explain the `igPivotView`™ control and its use.

`igPivotView` is a two-panel control that combines a pivot grid and a data selector separated with a splitter. It is a construction of three individual components – [igPivotGrid](/igpivotgrid/igpivotgrid.mdx)™ , [igPivotDataSelector](/igpivotdataselector/igpivotdataselector.mdx)™, and [igSplitter](/igsplitter/igsplitter.mdx)™ – assembled together to provide in one place all needed tools for manipulating multidimensional (OLAP data) in a pivot grid.
